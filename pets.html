Reptiles and Amphibians

Brookfield Garden and Pet Centre caters a vast array of reptiles and amphibians, including snakes, lizards, frogs, spiders and many more species. All reptiles and amphibians are cared for by our highly motivated and experienced team, ensuring they are equiped with the best equipment at all times. We also have a large selected of glass cabinets, UV lights, heat sources, habitat and vegetation available to help new pet owners eqiup them home in a proper fashion, all of which are at a reasonable price.

Small Mammals

The Brookfield Garden and Pet Centre is home to a wide variety of small mammals, including: hamsters, guinea pigs, rabbits and mice. All small mammals at Brookfield Garden and Pet Centre are looking for a forever home, and are on display at the small mammal sector. Equipment to aid in the care of small mammals is also available, including cages, food and toys.

View our selection of cats which are currently looking for a forever home! All cats at Brookfield Garden and Pet Centre are rescue or gifted pets, and are actively looking for a friendly family to take them home

Birds

Many of everyone's favourite bird species are available at Brookfield Garden and Pet Centre, including budgies, exotic parrots and parakeets, cockatiels and canaries. All of the equipment necessary to care for all of our bird species is available at Brookfield Garden and Pet Centre, including a wide range of cages, toys and accessories. Our experienced staff members are also on hand to provide support when choosing the bird species and finding suitable equipment.
